FAQs

How long does the battery last on a single charge?

Our wireless earbuds provide up to 6-10 hours of playtime on a full charge, with an additional 52 hours available through the charging case.

How do I pair the earbuds with my device?

Simply open the charging case, and the earbuds will enter pairing mode automatically. Go to your device's Bluetooth settings, select the earbuds, and connect.

Are these earbuds compatible with my phone?

Yes! These earbuds are compatible with all Bluetooth-enabled devices, including iOS and Android smartphones, tablets, and laptops.

Do the earbuds support noise cancellation?

Yes, they feature noise reduction technology to minimize background noise and enhance your listening experience.

Are the earbuds waterproof?

The earbuds are water-resistant, making them suitable for workouts and light rain, but they are not designed for swimming or full submersion.
